What is Bitcoin?

It's digital money that works over the internet.

Why was it invented?

To let people send cash securely over the internet anywhere in the world almost instantly, without fees and without having to share personal information with third parties. For an in depth explanation you can read the original white paper published by the inventor of Bitcoin Satoshi Nakamoto.
